What is the problem?

AI systems such as facial recognition technologies are shown to be biased against some demographic groups. One reason is that these systems haven’t seen enough diverse images to learn from. Most of the image datasets that we currently use for building facial recognition technologies primarily contain images of light-skinned male individuals. No wonder our systems are biased!

How can I help solve this problem?

We need your selfie images to make a diverse facial image dataset.

Start without reading more!

Our Effort to Improve Fairness

The current facial image datasets, not only lack diversity in important aspects such as age, gender, race, and skin tone, but they are also often not ethically sourced. As a group of scientists who work on ethical AI, with your help, we want to build the largest ethically sourced and diverse facial image dataset. Your images are collected only for research purposes and we ask you to self-identify your demographical attributes.


Who are we?

Get to know us better

We are a group of scientists at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems in Germany. With the help of the legal and ethical team at the Max Planck Society, we have made sure that your data is collected and saved securely, according to the latest EU standards.
